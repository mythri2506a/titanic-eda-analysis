# 🚢 Titanic Survival Analysis — EDA

## Overview
Complete Exploratory Data Analysis of the Titanic dataset.
Answered 15 key questions about passenger survival patterns
using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

Built as part of my AI/ML learning journey.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Overall survival rate: **38.4%**
- Women survived at **74%** vs men at **19%**
- 1st class passengers survived at **63%** vs 3rd class at **24%**
- Children under 12 had a **58%** survival rate
- Passengers travelling alone had only **30%** survival rate
- A 1st class woman had a **97%** chance of survival

---

## ❓ 15 Questions Answered

1. What is the overall survival rate?
2. How did survival differ by gender?
3. How did survival differ by passenger class?
4. Did age affect chances of survival?
5. What was the average fare per class?
6. Did family size affect survival?
7. Which port of embarkation had the best survival?
8. Which passenger titles (Mr, Mrs, Miss) survived most?
9. What is the correlation between age, fare and survival?
10. Did travelling alone hurt survival chances?
11. Who bought the top 10 most expensive tickets?
12. Were children under 12 prioritised for lifeboats?
13. Did cabin deck level affect survival?
14. Combined survival heatmap — Gender × Class
15. What is the profile of the most likely survivor?
